about MediRide

MediRide is a pioneering medical application dedicated to transforming emergency medical response in Nigeria. By harnessing the power of advanced GPS technology and a network of certified medical vehicles and personnel, MediRide provides rapid and reliable ambulance services, ensuring timely medical intervention in critical situations. We connects users with the nearest available ambulance with just a tap, facilitating swift transportation to hospitals while transmitting essential medical information ahead to prepare hospital staff for incoming patients.

our mission

At MediRide, commit to revolutionizing emergency medical response in Nigeria by providing rapid, reliable, and accessible ambulance services through cutting-edge technology. We are dedicated to reducing response times and improving patient outcomes by connecting users with certified medical vehicles and personnel in real time. Our commitment to innovation, efficiency, and compassion drives us to ensure that every individual receives the critical care they need, when they need it most, ultimately transforming the landscape of emergency healthcare in our nation.

our vission

Our vision at MediRide is to create a Nigeria where timely and efficient emergency medical care is accessible to everyone, regardless of their location. We aspire to lead the way in transforming emergency healthcare through technological innovation, ensuring that every life-threatening situation receives an immediate and effective response. By fostering a seamless integration of emergency services and healthcare providers, we envision a future where preventable deaths are drastically reduced, and every community enjoys the peace of mind that comes with reliable emergency medical support.
